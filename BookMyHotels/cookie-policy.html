Cookie Policy (GetmeRooms)

GetmeRooms wants that the access made to the services offered by us is streamlined as well as promising for the Visitor/User. In order to pursue the information, GetmeRooms incorporates cookies and Similar Technologies for enhancing the user experience with respect to hotel reservations and website security.

Cookies and Its Affects:

Cookies are the hidden text files that contain the behavior and the usage and are uploaded to the devices as and when the User/Visitor visits the website. These files get stored into the internet browser, which is then triggered sooner as the User/Visitor revisits the website. The purpose behind the triggering of cookies is to catch the recognition of the device from which the User/Visitor is communicating or browsing. Cookies help in enhancing the experience of the users by offering personalised content. GetmeRooms are also quick executable programs used for remembering the login details as a result the User/Visitor does not have to re-enter the information again and again for using the services of GetmeRooms. There are also cookies that help in making the understanding of the content clearer.

Cookies and the Types:

a) Strictly Necessary Cookies – These types of cookies are essential because without them the normal functioning of website is hampered strictly. The cookies respond only against the actions from User/Visitor. Such actions may either relate to correcting the privacy preferences, form filling or logging. The internet browser can be set to either put the cookies either into alert or blocking mode with respect to these cookies, as the result a few areas shall not work. Strictly Necessary Cookies shall not be able to store any type of personally identifiable information from the User/Visitor end.

b) Performance Cookies – The cookies provide an advantage to GetmeRooms to tally and calculate the visits as well as the traffic visiting for measuring as well as improving website performance. The cookies will guide the GetmeRooms through the extent of popularity of the pages as well as the manner of browsing by the User/Visitor. The information collected herewith by the cookies is gathered and anonymous in nature. In the event where cookies are stopped, we will have no information or incidence as to when the User/Visitor visited the website and hence could not monitor the outcome.

c) Functional Cookies These types of cookies when triggered will increase the personalisation as well as functional efforts. The cookies are triggered by the User/Visitor and/or by the third party providers, such as the hotel service providers, listed on web pages. If the cookies are not executed, there are chances where either few or rest of services stop from the functioning in a systematic order.

d) Targeting Cookies The type of cookies are triggered from our website by the advertising partners. The cookies are put to use by the companies for building personal profile of the User/Visitor and show adverts concerning their interests on other websites. Such type of cookies do not capture the personal; information, but instead classify and recognise the browser and internet device experience of the User/ Visitor. If the cookies are set to off, the User/Visitor shall either less or no advertising in the target markets.
